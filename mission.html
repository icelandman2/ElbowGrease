Our Mission Statement:

"Colatris enables app developers and stakeholders to outsource their entire internationalization effort within literal minutes. Our in-context mobile translation system is a lightweight solution that claims responsibility for multi-lingual support and global market reach. We believe that our customers should focus on what they do best... shipping quality product on schedule. Maintenance of multiple versions of content, further complicated by lingual intricacies, is a concern that dilutes attention from core product and business objectives. The heavy, and often overwhelming, task of servicing content translation and meeting the needs of an increasingly diverse user base is a pain point that we intend to remove from the situation entirely."


-Colatris Team

Goals

How we plan to redefine the experience of internationalization...

1.

Provide a full, end-to-end system for handling internationalization initiatives and servicing of a global audience, all the while simplifying the development and maintenance process. Think click-to-localize.

2.

Enable app developers/ stakeholders to “set and forget” through implementation of the SDK and subscription to the Colatris service. Watch in amazement as your 'base' language is automatically mirrored by a multitude of supported languages. Worry about the core product in it's native language. We'll handle the rest.

3.

Absolutely minimize scaling headache and enable serving of new translations/content out-of-band and without an update to the store! Colatris gives you the opportunity to serve content to your end users dynamically with the click of a button.

4.

Provide affordable, quality translations sourced from an educated group of smartphone users, submitting translation in their fluent tongue and on their own devices. Colatris' commitment is to represent our customer's message and voice as clearly and accurately as possible.
